Join Us to Help Shape the Future of Your High Street

Posted on: 20th February 2026

The regeneration of high streets across the West Midlands presents a major opportunity not just for physical renewal, but for social and economic transformation — particularly for young people.

If aligned properly with employability and skills strategies, high street regeneration can become a live pipeline into work.

Rather than viewing redevelopment purely as retail or infrastructure investment, local authorities and partners can embed skills and employment outcomes directly into regeneration plans.

Event Details:

Event: Growing the High Street

Date: Thursday 12 March

Time: 9am to 4pm

Location: The Bond in Digbeth
