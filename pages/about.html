Our Mission

Compass Consult exists to support private, public, and third-sector organisations to ‘find their way’ within the UK employability and skills sectors.

We understand the pressures of delivery, funding compliance, stakeholder scrutiny and performance outcomes -- because we’ve worked at every level of the system.

We don’t just advise. We work alongside you to implement solutions that deliver measurable impact for UK citizens.

Strategic Expertise

Whether launching a new programme or strengthening governance, we provide clear direction.

Practical Delivery

Hands-on support for mobilisation, bid management, and operational excellence.

Innovative Thinking

Helping organisations transform and evolve to meet new challenges.
